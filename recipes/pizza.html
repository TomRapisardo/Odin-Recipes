Margherita Pizza Recipe

Margherita Pizza in a Skillet

Delicious Margherita pizza recipe loaded with melted fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes,

and herbaceous basil leaves. Baking in a cast iron pan at a very high temperature ensures a crispy golden crust.


A fresh homemade pan pizza is always better tasting than ordering from delivery, and you can’t go wrong with a classic combo

like a Margherita pizza. Using a cast iron skillet is similar to cooking with a pizza stone, as this material is excellent at retaining

heat which helps to create a crunchy crust.

Ingredient List

    1 tablespoon olive oil, plus more for brushing
    1 pound pizza dough, store-bought or homemade
    ½ cup tomato sauce, or pizza sauce
    4 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, cut into 8 slices
    2 ripe tomatoes, cocktail, Campari, or Roma, about ten ¼" thick slices
    10 fresh basil leaves
    kosher salt, as needed for seasoning
    black pepper, as needed for seasoning

Steps

    Preheat the Oven – If using store-bought pizza dough, allow it to sit at room temperature for 30 to 45 minutes. Place the oven rack in the center position and preheat to 475ºF (246ºC).
